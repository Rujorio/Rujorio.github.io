Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Infinite Chess ("we," "the Game") handles your information when you play the game via Discord or the web.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

  • We do not store your Discord username or User ID.
  • We do not have a central database to store game history or moves.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics (like Google Analytics).

2. Peer-to-Peer Networking

Infinite Chess uses PeerJS to connect players directly. When you share your "Peer ID" with a friend, a direct connection is established between your browser and theirs. Game data (moves) is sent over this encrypted connection and is never seen or stored by us.

3. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect data, the following third-party services are used to make the game work:

  • GitHub Pages: Hosts the game files. They may collect standard web server logs (IP addresses) for security purposes.
  • Discord: If played as a Discord Activity, Discord's own Privacy Policy applies to your use of their platform.
  • PeerJS Cloud: Used briefly to "handshake" or find your friend's connection. They do not store your game data.

4. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, the game is safe for users of all ages. However, we encourage all players to follow Discord's age requirements.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we ever add a "Leaderboard" or "Accounts" feature that requires data storage, we will update this policy and notify users within the game UI.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach out via the GitHub repository where this project is hosted.
